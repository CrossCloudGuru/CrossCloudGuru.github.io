### Graph Top Down

```mermaid

    A-->B;
    A-->C;
    B-->D;
    C-->D;
```

### Graph Down to Top

```mermaid
graph TD;
    D-->C;
    D-->B;
    B-->A;
    C-->B;
```

### Graph Left Right

```mermaid
graph LR;
  A[Hello!] --> B{This};
  B --> C(is a);
  C --> D((Flowchart!));
```
