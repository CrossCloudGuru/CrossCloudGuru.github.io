### Graph Top Down

```mermaid
graph TD;
    A-->B;
    A-->C;
    B-->D;
    C-->D;
```

---

### Graph Left Right

​```mermaid
  graph LR;
  A[Hello!] --> B{This}
  B --> C(is a)
  C --> D((Flowchart!))
​```

---

### Sequence Diagram

```mermaid
graph sequenceDiagram;
Alice->>John: Hello John, how are you?;
loop Healthcheck;
    John->>John: Fight against hypochondria;
end;
Note right of John: Rational thoughts!;
John-->>Alice: Great!;
John->>Bob: How about you?;
Bob-->>John: Jolly good!;
```
